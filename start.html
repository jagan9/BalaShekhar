About Bala

Android, Java Developer, and Teacher

I am the founder of Build Apps With bala, where I teach people Java and Android Development.

I was born and raised in saduvelly, a country located in Asia bordered on the North by the Indian Ocean. Growing up I was obsessed with how things work. At a young age, I was able to disassemble VCR tapes and fix them, which ended up being my first business ( if you are interested in hearing more about this, message me).

Later, in 2017 when I was 18, I was given an amazing opportunity to come to the IIT as an exchange student. I lived in Hyderabad, Telangana for a year. It was an extraordinary experience which opened a lot of doors for me.

In 2018, another opportunity came knocking. This time I returned to the IIT for college while living in the Jharkhand.

After graduating college, I worked for a company, building Android and iOS mobiles apps as well as web applications.

In late 2018, my brother inspired me to start teaching online. Up to that point, I had never realized how much I love teaching. It brings me joy and a sense of purpose.

I've taught more than 40,000 students from over 170 countries how to program in Java and how to build Android apps.

It's humbling to hear students stories: how they have taken my courses and were then to get their dream jobs building apps. Or how some students were struggling to understand Java Programming concepts, and after taking my course, those "hard-to-grasp" concepts were finally well understood.

Teaching is my passion. My mission is to help 1 Million students reach their goal of becoming the best developers they can be. I want to make you, a well-rounded developer.

Thank you so much for being here. It means the world to me!

Bala Shekhar
